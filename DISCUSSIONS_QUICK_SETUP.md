# GitHub Discussions - Quick Setup (5 Minutes)

Copy-paste ready opening posts for each discussion category.

---

## Step 1: Enable Discussions

1. Click **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Features**
3. Check **Discussions**
4. Go to **Discussions** tab (now visible in your repo)

---

## Step 2: Create 4 Discussion Categories

Click **New Discussion** and create these (in order):

## Step 3: Engage

**First Week:**
1. Respond to every new discussion within 24 hours
2. Ask follow-up questions (shows genuine interest)
3. Thank people for their feedback

**Ongoing:**
1. Share releases in Announcements
2. Feature users in Show and Tell
3. Act on feedback - show ideas ‚Üí action
4. Monthly recap

## Pro Tips

- **Pin the Announcements post** - Keep it at the top

- **Celebrate contributors** - Feature them publicly
